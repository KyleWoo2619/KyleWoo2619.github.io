About Me

I am a senior game design student at UCF with a passion for building engaging gameplay and leading teams toward clear goals. My focus lies in creating systems that feel intuitive for players while keeping production organized and achievable for teams.

As a producer, I’ve managed projects across Unity and Unreal, using Scrum and Jira to keep milestones on track. As a designer, I enjoy exploring player motivation, level progression, and boss encounters that challenge both mechanically and narratively. As a leader, I’ve guided teams of various sizes by promoting collaboration, conflict resolution, and clear communication.

I thrive in environments where creativity meets structure — where ideas become polished, playable experiences delivered on time.
